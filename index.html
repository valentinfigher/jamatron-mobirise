JAmatron

A new way to listen to your favorite music, together with friends and fans.

Find out more
We are trying to revolutionize collaborative music streaming.

Jamatron takes listening parties to a whole new level. Jam to your favorite songs or to a fresh out the oven release from one of your favorite artists with this innovative new social music service.

Jamatron connects to your everyday-use streaming service provider and allows you and your friends to jam together, in real time, to the same music, from a distance.

FAQs

  • When will Jamatron be released?
    We don't have a release schedule set in stone. We are constantly working on the app and hoping to bring it to you soon.
  • Can I join the early beta?
    We currently aren't looking for new users for the beta but check our social media profiles every now and then, we might do random access giveaways to the app.
  • Will *insert artist name* get a Jamatron album release?
    We are closely in talks with music labels in hopes of making album releases a regular thing. Right now the only artists that can have Jamatron album releases are independent artists.
